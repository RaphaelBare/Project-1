Our idea :

FitGenius is a digital platform that connects users with certified personal trainers for customized fitness plans. You can choose from a variety of trainers based on their specialty and experience, and then work with them to create a personalized workout and nutrition plan tailored to your goals.

How to choose?

This is obviously the easiest thing to understand on our platform. You can browse through the different trainers on the website and read their bios and customer reviews to find one that fits your needs. You can also book a consultation call with a trainer to discuss your goals and preferences before committing to a plan.
